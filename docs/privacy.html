Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

PuppyTrack ("the app") is developed by Aykut Yıldırım. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Email address, used to create and authenticate your account via Supabase.
  • Dog profiles: Name, breed size, age, and home type you enter for your dogs.
  • Training logs: Bathroom event records (type, result, timestamp) you log manually.
  • Feeding logs: Water and food intake records you log manually.
  • Notification preferences: Stored locally on your device (AsyncStorage).
  • Consent choices: Whether you opted in to anonymous data sharing, stored locally.

2. Optional: Anonymous Data Sharing

After your 10th log entry, the app may ask whether you'd like to share anonymous behavioral patterns (e.g., "success at 7 AM, 3-month-old, medium breed"). This is strictly opt-in. If you agree:

  • No name, email, or personally identifying information is ever shared.
  • Only aggregated behavioral patterns are collected.
  • You can withdraw consent at any time in Profile → App Settings.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's core features (logging, predictions, charts).
  • To send local push notifications for bathroom reminders (if you enable them).
  • To improve training predictions for all users (only if you opt in to anonymous sharing).

4. Data Storage

Your account and training data are stored in Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is associated with your account and is not shared with third parties. Notification preferences and consent flags are stored locally on your device and never transmitted.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and database. Supabase Privacy Policy

The app does not contain advertising and does not use advertising SDKs.

7. Children

PuppyTrack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
